Place your self-recorded source video clips here.

Files in this directory are git-ignored (see .gitignore).
Do NOT commit personal video/audio data to version control.

Expected files for Phase 1:
  - your_clip.wav      : 10–30 second audio of yourself speaking English (for ASR + voice cloning)
  - your_video.mp4     : 30–90 second video of yourself speaking English (for lip-sync)

Recording guidelines:
  - Near-frontal face angle (avoid profiles — Wav2Lip artifacts increase on profiles)
  - Good lighting, minimal background noise
  - Varied emotional tones across clips (calm, excited, serious, neutral)
  - 30–90 seconds per clip (longer clips are out of scope for this project)
